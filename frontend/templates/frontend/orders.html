ORDER ONLINE

Treatments will be supplied to your location once they have been approved by our Online Doctor(s.). You can order the treatments or tests that have been approved for you if you have asked the doctor to recommend them.

How does Order Online work?


  1. You will order via phone call and collect in store.
  2. Your order will be ready for collection from your selected store the next business day.
  3. You will be contacted via email or SMS once your purchase/s are ready to be collected.
  4. Please do not come to the store without receiving a confirmation notification in order to collect your purchase.

To place an order call these numbers 0772286424 Mtn or 0759618819 Airtel.

How will i receive my order?


You can either collect your order from any First Pharmacy store for free, or have it delivered to your home address the next working day at a cost.

What are the benefits of this service?


  1. Speedy turnaround time
  2. No additional delivery fees
  3. Available 7 days a week
  4. Available in most stores country wide

How long does it take to get my order approved?


On a weekday, between the hours 7am to 10pm, our pharmacists aim to assess your questionnaire within one hour. At a weekend, they aim to do this as quick as possible.
